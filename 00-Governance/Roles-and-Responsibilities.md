# Roles and Responsibilities

Status: Draft for WG discussion
Date proposed: 07.06.2026

## Translators

Translate assigned handbook text into Arabic using the approved glossary and Arabic style guide.

## Reviewers

Review Arabic language quality, clarity, grammar, readability, and consistency of style.

## SE Reviewers

Review systems engineering meaning, technical correctness, and alignment with INCOSE terminology.

## Glossary Curator

Maintains the official glossary, records terminology disagreements, and ensures approved terms are applied consistently across chapters.
