# Translation Principles v0.1

Status: For WG approval
Date proposed: 07.06.2026

## Principle 1

Translate concepts, not words.

## Principle 2

Consistency is more important than perfect wording.

## Principle 3

Use one approved Arabic term per English term.

## Principle 4

Every chapter follows the same glossary.

## Principle 5

Disagreements are documented and decided later, not during translation.
