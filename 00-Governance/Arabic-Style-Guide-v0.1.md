# Arabic Style Guide v0.1

Status: For WG approval
Date proposed: 07.06.2026

## Question 1: Formal Arabic only?

Recommendation: Yes.

Use formal Arabic for handbook translation. Avoid colloquial regional expressions unless a note explicitly discusses regional usage.

## Question 2: English term in brackets on first occurrence?

Recommendation: Yes.

Example:

هندسة النظم (Systems Engineering)

After the first occurrence, use the approved Arabic term only unless clarity requires repeating the English term.

## Question 3: How should acronyms be handled?

Recommendation: Keep acronyms in English.

Example:

هندسة النظم المعتمدة على النماذج (MBSE)

Use [[Acronyms]] and [[Acronyms.base]] as the working acronym list.
