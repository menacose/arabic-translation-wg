# INCOSE MENA Arabic Glossary

Status: v0.1 for WG approval
Date proposed: 07.06.2026

This is the official working glossary for the MENA Arabic translation of the INCOSE Systems Engineering Handbook.

Goal for the kickoff session: approve 20-30 terms, not 500.

Structured selection matrices for all terms are tracked in [[Term-Selection-Matrices]].

Editable per-term notes are tracked in [[Glossary.base]] and stored in `01-Glossary/Terms/`. Source definitions are also stored in those same term notes.
