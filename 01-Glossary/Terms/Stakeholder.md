# Stakeholder

Arabic:
صاحب مصلحة

Status:
Proposed

## Source Definition

A party having a right, share, or claim in a system or in its possession of characteristics that meet that party's needs and expectations.

Source:
INCOSE Systems Engineering Handbook, Appendix C
